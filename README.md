#  Customer Experience Intelligence Dashboard (Power BI)

##  Project Overview  
In today’s digital-first banking environment, customer experience is no longer just a service metric — it is a strategic growth driver.

This project tells a compelling story hidden within customer complaints, satisfaction levels, operational inefficiencies, and churn behavior across banking channels in Nigeria.

Using Power BI, I transformed raw complaint and service data into an end-to-end analytical narrative, answering a critical question:

Why are customer complaints rising despite heavy digitalization — and how is this impacting churn?

---

##  The Story Flow (From Insight to Action)

This dashboard is not just a collection of visuals — it is a guided journey across four analytical layers:

---

###  1. Executive Customer Experience Overview  
“What is happening?”

We begin with a high-level snapshot of customer experience:

- Rising complaint volumes across months  
- 40% low customer satisfaction rate  
- 34% churn rate signaling retention risk  
- Regional and channel distribution of complaints  

At a glance, the organization appears operationally active but experientially strained.

---

###  2. Digital Root Cause Analysis  
“Where is the problem coming from?”

We drill into digital channels — expected to improve experience but revealing a different reality:

- 41% of complaints originate from digital platforms  
- High frustration rate across digital interactions  
- Elevated transaction failure rates (especially POS & Internet Banking)  
- Longer wait times than expected for “instant” channels  

Digital transformation is driving volume, but not satisfaction — exposing hidden inefficiencies.

---

###  3. Operational Bottlenecks  
“What is breaking internally?”

We uncover the operational friction behind poor customer experience:

- Average resolution time exceeds optimal thresholds  
- 35% SLA breach rate  
- Staff attitude ratings impacting satisfaction  
- Wait time directly correlates with declining satisfaction  

The issue is not just digital — it is process inefficiency and service delivery gaps.

---

###  4. Churn Risk Drivers  
“What is the business impact?”

Finally, we connect experience to business outcomes:

- 685 customers identified as high churn risk  
- Churn strongly linked to low satisfaction  
- Regional churn disparities highlight location-based issues  
- SLA breaches and wait times directly influence churn  

Customer experience failures are translating into real revenue risk.

---

##  Key Insights

- Digital channels contribute the highest complaint volume but do not guarantee better experience  
- Customer satisfaction is a leading indicator of churn  
- Operational inefficiencies (SLA breaches, wait time) are core drivers of dissatisfaction  
- Churn is not random — it is predictable and preventable
